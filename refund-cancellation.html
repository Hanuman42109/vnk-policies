Refunds and Cancellations

Last updated: June 2026

1. Cancellation Window

Customers may request cancellation while an order is pending or confirmed. Once an order is preparing, out for delivery, ready for pickup, delivered, or picked up, cancellation may be declined if products have already been packed or dispatched.

2. How to Cancel

Use the Request Cancellation option in the Orders screen when available, or contact the store immediately with your order ID. Cancellation is not final until the store confirms it in the app or by direct communication.

3. Online Payment Refunds

For approved cancellations or eligible refunds on online payments, refunds are initiated through Razorpay to the original payment method. Refunds usually reflect within 5-7 business days after initiation, depending on the payment provider or bank.

4. Cash and Pay-at-Store Orders

For cash on delivery or pay-at-store orders, no online refund is needed unless an advance payment was collected online. Any cash adjustment will be handled directly by the store.

5. Damaged, Missing, or Wrong Items

Report damaged, missing, expired, or wrong items as soon as possible after delivery or pickup. The store may request order details, photos, or product return before approving replacement, store credit, or refund.

6. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds may be declined for incorrect customer details, unavailable customer at delivery, misuse of cancellation requests, opened or used products where return is not appropriate, or issues reported after an unreasonable delay.
