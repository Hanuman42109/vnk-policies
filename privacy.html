Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Vinukonda Kirana. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Name and contact details, including phone number and email address.
  • Delivery address and saved address details.
  • Order history, order status, preferences, and support requests.
  • Payment references and transaction status from Razorpay. We do not store card, UPI, net banking, wallet, or payment credential details.
  • Device information, app usage data, and push notification token where required for app functionality.

This information is collected only when you provide it during registration, checkout, order management, support, or normal app usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process and deliver orders, send order updates and notifications, improve our app and services, contact you regarding orders or support, maintain account and order history, prevent misuse or fraud, and comply with legal or business obligations.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services and related app infrastructure. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Data is transmitted using secure connections where supported by the service provider.

4. Payment Information

Payments are processed through Razorpay, a secure payment gateway. We do not store card details, UPI IDs, bank details, wallet credentials, or other payment credentials on our servers. Payment transactions are handled by Razorpay, and we store only order and payment reference information needed for order confirmation, refunds, records, and support.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share limited information with:

  • Delivery personnel, to fulfill orders using name, phone number, address, and order details.
  • Firebase or Google services, for authentication, data storage, and app functionality.
  • Razorpay, for online payment processing, verification, and refunds.
  • Service providers used for hosting, notifications, email, security, or support.
  • Legal, tax, regulatory, or law enforcement authorities where required by applicable law.

We do not share your data with advertisers.

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications about order status, delivery updates, cancellations, refunds, offers, and app updates. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to personal data we hold about you, correction of inaccurate data, deletion of your account and associated personal data, or opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep account, address, order, and payment reference records only as long as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, meet legal obligations, and maintain business records. You may request account and data deletion from the app support/contact section or by emailing us. Some order or transaction records may be retained where required for tax, accounting, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating this page or through the app where appropriate. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
